Configuration

Example nineml.properties file:

verbose=true
pretty-print=false
ignore-trailing-whitespace=true
graphviz=/usr/local/bin/dot

Read from .nineml.properties in the current directory, or nineml.properties in the same directory as the CoffeePot jar file, or from the classpath.
